Overweight Teen Diet Plan

Breakfast

Option Food
1 Whole wheat oatmeal with berries and a sprinkle of nuts, veggie omelet with spinach, mushrooms, and cheese, whole wheat toast with avocado and a slice of lean protein (turkey breast or Canadian bacon)
2 Greek yogurt with a small amount of granola and chopped fruit, whole wheat pancakes with fruit and a dollop of low-fat yogurt, scrambled eggs with whole wheat toast and a side of fruit

Mid-Morning Snack

Option Food
1 Sliced pear with low-fat cheese, baby carrots with hummus
2 Small protein smoothie made with banana, spinach, and milk (protein powder optional), handful of almonds

Lunch

Option Food
1 Large salad with grilled chicken or fish, whole wheat pita bread, and light vinaigrette dressing
2 Lentil soup with a side salad (olive oil and vinegar dressing), whole wheat crackers

Afternoon Snack

Option Food
1 Cottage cheese with chopped vegetables, small apple slices
2 Sliced cucumber with a tablespoon of nut butter

Dinner

Option Food
1 Baked salmon with roasted sweet potato and steamed broccoli
2 Chicken stir-fry with brown rice and mixed vegetables, side salad with light dressing
