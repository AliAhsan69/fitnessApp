Fit Teen Diet Plan

Breakfast

Option Food
1 Whole wheat toast with scrambled eggs and vegetables (spinach, mushrooms, peppers), banana, glass of low-fat milk or fortified plant-based milk
2 Greek yogurt with berries and a sprinkle of granola, oatmeal with nuts and seeds, whole wheat pancakes with fruit

Mid-Morning Snack

Option Food
1 Apple slices with almond butter, vegetable sticks with hummus
2 Protein smoothie made with banana, spinach, protein powder, and milk, yogurt parfait with granola and fruit

Lunch

Option Food
1 Lean protein salad (grilled chicken, fish, or tofu) with whole grain bread or wrap, vegetables, and light dressing
2 Whole wheat pasta with marinara sauce and lean ground turkey or lentils, side salad with olive oil and vinegar

Afternoon Snack

Option Food
1 Cottage cheese with fruit and a sprinkle of granola, trail mix with nuts, seeds, and dried fruit (limited sugar)
2 Whole wheat crackers with cheese slices, banana with peanut butter

Dinner

Option Food
1 Baked salmon or chicken breast, brown rice, roasted vegetables
2 Lentil soup with whole grain bread, stir-fry with lean protein and vegetables over brown rice
