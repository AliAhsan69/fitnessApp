Underweight Adult Diet Plan

Breakfast

Option Food
1 Whole grain toast with avocado spread, scrambled eggs, banana, glass of whole milk or fortified plant-based milk
2 Greek yogurt with mixed berries and honey, whole grain cereal with milk, handful of nuts (almonds, walnuts, or cashews)

Mid-Morning Snack

Option Food
1 Apple slices with peanut butter, whole grain crackers with cheese
2 Smoothie made with banana, spinach, peanut butter, and milk, granola bar

Lunch

Option Food
1 Grilled chicken or tofu wrap with whole wheat tortilla, lettuce, tomato, and hummus, carrot and cucumber sticks with hummus, quinoa salad with mixed vegetables
2 Lentil soup, whole grain roll, mixed green salad with olive oil and lemon dressing

Afternoon Snacks

Option Food
1 Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks, Handful of trail mix (nuts, seeds, and dried fruits)
2 Whole grain toast with almond butter, Banana smoothie with milk and honey

Evening Snacks

Option Food
1 Greek yogurt with sliced strawberries, Whole grain crackers with hummus
2 Cottage cheese with pear slices, Handful of almonds

Dinner

Option Food
1 Baked salmon or trout, Sweet potato mash, Steamed broccoli
2 Whole grain pasta with marinara sauce and lean ground turkey or tofu, Spinach salad with tomatoes, cucumbers, and feta cheese
