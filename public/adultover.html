Overweight Adult Diet Plan

Breakfast

Option Food
1 Vegetable omelette with egg whites, whole grain toast, sliced tomatoes, and avocado
2 Greek yogurt with sliced strawberries, whole grain cereal, and a small handful of almonds

Mid-Morning Snack

Option Food
1 Carrot sticks with hummus
2 Low-fat cottage cheese with apple slices

Lunch

Option Food
1 Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, and balsamic vinaigrette dressing
2 Quinoa and black bean salad with grilled vegetables

Afternoon Snacks

Option Food
1 Plain popcorn (air-popped)
2 Small apple with a tablespoon of natural peanut butter

Evening Snacks

Option Food
1 Vegetable sticks (carrots, celery, bell peppers) with hummus
2 Low-fat yogurt with a handful of berries

Dinner

Option Food
1 Grilled fish with roasted vegetables (such as broccoli, cauliflower, and carrots)
2 Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables served over brown rice
